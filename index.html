SINGLE SERVE (DOMAIN)

A single-serving site (SSS) is a website composed of a single page with a dedicated domain name and which serves only one purpose.

Create a standalone website which expresses a single idea, thought, or answer to a question. Consider how the design of the site reinforces the underlying concept, and how the title (and corresponding domain name) prepares or prompts your user to engage or respond.

Your site should be published to a domain name of your choosing. It is recommended, though not required, that you use Namecheap to register your domain name:
